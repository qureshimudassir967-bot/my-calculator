About BMI

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple screening tool that estimates body fat based on height and weight. It does not diagnose body fatness or health.

Formula:

Metric: BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]²
Imperial: BMI = 703 × weight (lb) / [height (in)]²

WHO Categories:
• Underweight: < 18.5
• Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
• Overweight: 25 – 29.9
• Obese: ≥ 30
