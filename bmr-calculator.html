What is BMR?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic life functions (breathing, circulation, cell production) while at rest.

Formulas:

Mifflin-St Jeor:
Men: (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) + 5
Women: (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) - 161

Harris-Benedict:
Men: 88.362 + (13.397 × weight) + (4.799 × height) - (5.677 × age)
Women: 447.593 + (9.247 × weight) + (3.098 × height) - (4.330 × age)
