Understanding BMI: What It Means for Your Health

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple screening tool that estimates body fat based on height and weight. The formula is: BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)².

A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy for most adults. Our BMI Calculator supports both metric and imperial units.

How to Calculate Loan Payments with the EMI Formula

The Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for a loan can be calculated using: EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1) where P = principal, r = monthly interest rate, n = number of months.
