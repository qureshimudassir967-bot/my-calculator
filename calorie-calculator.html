How It Works

This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), then adjusts for activity level to find your maintenance calories.

• Weight Loss: Maintenance – 500 kcal
• Muscle Gain: Maintenance + 300–500 kcal

⚠️ These are estimates. Individual needs may vary based on body composition, health, and metabolism.
